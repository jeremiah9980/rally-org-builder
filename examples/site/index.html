We don't recruit for this season — we develop for the next level. Operations are owned by directors so coaches own the field, and every athlete gets a real plan she's held to with care.

🧱 The Coaching Firewall

Parents may not pressure coaches about playing time, lineup, or family absences. Non-coaching concerns route to the Co-Directors. Coaches are protected from politics — and from disputes that aren't softball. Both directions of the firewall are enforced.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family-First Coverage

Coaches and staff are parents too. Their kids' lives don't compete with the team. When a coach has a family conflict, staff coverage keeps the field running. Schedules are surfaced monthly; absences are expected, planned for, and never criticized.
